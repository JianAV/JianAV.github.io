Glossary

Sus
A term derived from the popular game Among Us where you find the suspicious imposter in a group and vote them out. It is often used in substitute of the word "suspicious" among the younger generations, such as Millenials or Zoomers.
Example: "Yo, whatcha doing there huh? Why're you acting so sus kuh?"
Cringe
A term used to represent the feeling of displeasure either out of embarrassment or discomfort. It is also used to describe behavior that is deemed disturbing or laughable by the younger generations.
Example: "Damn bro, that's kinda cringe lmao"
